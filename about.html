  • Private astronauts, space tourists, or just someone looking to take a small vacation -whatever you choose to call yourself- we can all agree that space is the new and exciting frontier that all of humankind should get the chance to explore. With LIMITLESS, we give you a plethora of options to consider which route you wish to take in order to see the myseries of outer space.
  • In the beginning days, our company wasn't quite sure how to tackle this pipe dream of ours. That is, until we figured out what exactly we actually wanted to provide. We had decided that our company will focus on the excitement of "exploration" rather than "researching!" Space, in our eyes, was similar to a tourist attraction. Meaning, those who wish should be able to explore it with us and spectate the visuals of outer space.
  • With LIMITLESS, the only work you have to do is deciding on where you want to go in the cosmos, how you want to get there, and how long you plan to stay. You can leave the rest up to us! No need to worry about suits, food, or even fun past times, we've got you covered.
  • LIMITLESS is here to help you save as much money as you can on your exploration, while still having fun. We will always be upfront about our prices, so that you never have to wonder if it is too costly for you and your family. Check out our financing page to get a grasp on our pricing ranges and find the trip that is perfectly suited for you!
