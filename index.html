DESTINATIONS

UPPER ATMOSPHERE

Want to experience what the extraterrestrial life is like, while still being close to home? 100 km away from the Earth's surface, you will be able to see the gentle blue glow that emanates from the Earth's atmosphere. A relaxing view for all members of the family to enjoy. On special occasions, you might even catch a glimpse of the ethereal silver moon orbiting the planet we call home. A serene view that all should be able to check off on their bucket list.

MOON'S SURFACE

As NASA would describe our moon: "a beautiful blue marble with white swirls," and we at LIMITLESS find that it's almost the perfect description. With a 3-day flight to the moon, and a time you will never forget, you too can lay your eyes (and your camera) on our beautiful blue marble from the it's own surface. As well as play a bit of zero gravity moon golf while you're there!

EARTH'S ORBITAL

Perhaps you're not a fan of staying stationary or close to home? Looking for a tranquil vacation that will decrease the weight of your stresses? Why not coast our orbit in our top facilities! We keep our trained personnel nearby, provide all food and amenities one might need, and make frequent stops to view celestial life, all so you can relax as much as you wish without worry.

VEHICLES

In order to save us much money as we can for you, and for us, we have partnered up with Blue Origin, SpaceX, and Virgin Galactic to ensure the most cost friendly, and luxurious ride we can offer. We also provide our own vehicles, but we still like to ensure that the customer always has a cost efficient, safe, and vast choice regarding their prefered vehicle for traveling the cosmos.

PACKAGES

Perhaps you're unsure where to start your journey? Well we have some curated packages for you! All suits and other necessities will be provided by us. All you have to do is purchase the package that's the best fit for you.

COUPLE'S LUNAR GOLFING

Enjoy a week off with your significant other on the lunar surface with fun, food, and most importantly, golf! All vehicles and equipment provided by Limitless.

SINGLE ORBIT

Choose your vacation length, and spend that much coasting Earth's orbit while enjoying all of the benefits of zero gravity! Vehicles provided by Limitless.

QUICK FAMILY OUTING

Looking for something new to do with the family? Why not float in the Earth's atmosphere for a bit of fun for all sizes! Vehicles provided by Limitless.
