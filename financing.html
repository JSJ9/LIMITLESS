FINANCING

Financing can be a detrimental aspect of any idea, so we are here to breakdown the costs to help you understand why we are asking for the amount that we are.

We aren't going to lie to you and say these trips are cheap, because they aren't. Many expenses stem from the travel and suits alone, which is why we offer many different options for the purchaser in terms of vehicle providers. Prices can range anywhere from $250 thousand to tens of millions of dollars depending on the packages, activities, and how long you and your loved ones are in our top facilities. However, we have no set prices as each trip is customized for each of our costumers, so to get your planning started, please call (616)420-9994 to get your very own flight planner (and adviser).

Now, while each trip is costumized, there are still base prices depending on which vehicle provider you choose:




Blue Origin

Blue Origin, by Jeff Bezos, charges $100k for each minute in Earth's atmosphere. Meaning that for around $1 million, you get 10 minutes, 105 km away from Earth's surface.

Virgin Galactic

With Virgin Galactic, runned by Richard Branson, your price is based on time. Along with a deposit of $150k, a 90-minute trips costs $450k.

SpaceX

SpaceX, brought to you by Elon Musk, actually charges per person. The average cost in 2022 for a seat that goes beyond the atmosphere was $55 million per person.
