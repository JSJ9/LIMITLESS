LAUNCH & RECOVERY

L I M I T L E S S

1)

Before we can discuss launch and recovery, we must show you the blueprint of our Low Earth Orbital Rocket, or LEO for short. It was invented to withstand multiple uses and can carry up to 5 families, or around 25 people, in its orbiter.

2)

In order to save costs in the long term, we used top quality aerospace grade aluminum that is coated with heat shield tiles (made of silica fiber) and glass that protects against the Earth's harsh atmoshpere, making for a smooth return back to land. In the future we plan to use carbon composite structures as it's much sturdier and lighter. Since the lighter a rocket is, the less fuel it'll need to break through the Earth's atmosphere, resulting in overall lower costs per trip.

3)

Of course, depending on the vehicle the costumer chooses to go to space in, the launch path and style may vary. However, while we can not speak for others, we can explain what a launch would look like if you choose our very own ships. After everyone is loaded on and all of the safety procedures are gone through, the aluminum-lithium alloy tanks on our rockets blast you off of the surface. Next, the main engines stop near the Earth's atmosphere and the top of the rocket is dislodged from the rest, as the smaller tanks take control. Finally, the propelers on the remaining parts of the rocket allow you and the crew to move through space, cruising as you wish, while the rest of the rocket returns to a landing site. After your trip has concluded, the landing phase begins.

4)

When discussing the problem of landing, we can look to the ocean. Time and time again, bodies of water can be seen catching rockets or satilites from space. We plan to use a remote, but accessable part of the Gulf of Mexico so that landing is a pleasant ride, while also being safe. Alas, as always, there is some turbulence to be expected, but with our staff and top notch rocket interior, it will feel like a joyride in an old convertable on a warm summer day.

LIMITLESS is a fictional space travel company.
